# Tetra-X Cultural Conversion Engine

AI-native agency prototype that converts product truth into culturally resonant taglines.

Instead of translating marketing copy, the system aligns brand messaging with customer reviews, extracts the validated product truth, and generates market-ready taglines for different cultures (US and Japan).

Live demo: https://ai-assistant-toshitetra.replit.app/

What this demo shows

This prototype demonstrates two core ideas:

1. Reality Alignment  
Compare what the brand says with what customers actually value.

2. Cultural Transformation  
Express the same product truth differently depending on cultural storytelling style.

Example flow

Brand claim  
+ customer reviews  
→ validated product truth  
→ US tagline (direct, confident)  
→ JP tagline (subtle, emotional)

Why this matters

AI can generate content easily, but market-ready communication still requires cultural intelligence.

Brands often fail overseas not because of their product, but because their brand voice does not resonate in another culture.

This system shows how AI combined with consumer insight can produce culturally adapted messaging instead of simple translation.

Demo flow

1. Input a product page URL  
2. Extract brand messaging signals  
3. Extract consumer signals from reviews  
4. Identify the validated product truth  
5. Generate US and Japanese tagline proposals  
6. Visualize cultural expression differences

Cultural Lens

The system models cultural expression using multiple dimensions.  
The UI displays representative indicators:

Directness  
Emotion  
Figurative Expression  
Narrative Style

Vision

This project is an experimental prototype built during a hackathon.

It explores one idea related to Tetra-X.ai: converting validated product truth (from brand messaging and customer reviews) into culturally adapted taglines for different markets.
